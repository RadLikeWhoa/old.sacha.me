About Me

My name is Sacha Schmid. I'm a front-end developer from Switzerland, interested in both interface design and web development. I work at Schwabe in Muttenz, Switzerland.

Currently, I am studying Computer Science at the University of Applied Sciences and Arts Northwestern Switzerland in Brugg, Switzerland and am on track to get my bachelor's degree in 2017.

I have previously had experience in front-end development while working with private clients, small companies, and a large Swiss department store, as well as a multitude of projects at university. You can see some of that work on my Projects page. Clients enjoy working with me because I am a disciplined worker who likes to bring his own ideas into the project at hand.

About This Site
